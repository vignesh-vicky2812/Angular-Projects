General questions

I want to view my past payments?

Simply login at voizfonica.in and go to Billing section >> Last Payments to view details of last 3 payments made.

How do I contact customer care?

In case you wish to contact customer care, please call us at 121 for queries/requests and 198 for complaints.

I want to know about my current package?

check your current pack by going to My Account >> My Package

Popular Search

I want to receive a duplicate bill?

you can view/download/email your bills by simply logging on to airtel.in and clicking on Billing section. Your bills are available under Last Bill and Statements.

I want to know the details of my last 5 transactions?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Add your prepaid account on it using the ‘Add account’ link under the ‘my accounts’ section on the homepage. Tap on the prepaid account from the home screen to reach the account HomeScreen of your prepaid account. Select "Recharge History" and it shall reflect all the recharges done through the app.
  • Dial *121*7# from your prepaid mobile number to know the details of your previous 5 transactions.
  • Sign in to your account using your prepaid mobile number on airtel.in . Click on “Manage Services” and then “Usage Details”.

I want to change my bill plan?

Simply login at and go to Plan & Services >> Update Rental to change your bill plan. You may also change your bill plan by going to My Account >> Plan >> View Plan >> Change Plan.

I want to know my balance and validity?

To know the current balance and validity on your account, click here. You can also give a missed call to 81300-81300 from your registered mobile number to know your balance.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.
